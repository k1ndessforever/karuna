What we collect
✅Your email address used only to send an OTP. Never stored in plaintext. Never shared. Never used to contact you.
✅Your nickname chosen by you, anonymous. Could be "WardhaFeeder" or "Anonymous123". We don't care who you really are.
✅Your report content the animal, location text, photo if added. This is the whole point. It lives in the database so others can help.
✅Timestamp and language preference so we know when posts were made and what language to show them in.
we never collect
🚫Phone number never asked, never needed.
🚫Real name not now, not ever.
🚫Device fingerprint, browser info, IP address we do not run any tracking scripts.
🚫Location from your GPS you type your location in words. We never ask your device for coordinates.
🚫Behavioural data what you clicked, how long you stayed, what you searched. None of it. Ever.
🚫Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any ad tracking not installed. Not planned. Not negotiable.
limitations
⚠️Supabase stores your email it is a third-party service based in the USA. Their privacy policy applies. We use the minimum data possible with them.
⚠️Vercel hosts our code they may log request metadata (not content) as part of normal server operation.
⚠️OpenStreetMap tiles the map images load from OSM servers. OSM may log the tile requests. No personal data is attached to these.
⚠️AI analysis when you post a report, the text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for fraud checking. Anthropic's data policy applies to this processing.

Security

Are you really safe here?
answer

No platform is 100% safe. Anyone who tells you otherwise is lying. Here is what we do, what we don't do, and what the real risks are.

🔒

HTTPS Everywhere

All traffic between you and Karuna is encrypted. Nobody in the middle can read what you post or receive.

👤

Anonymous by Default

Your real identity is never stored. If Karuna's database were breached, attackers would find nicknames and hashed emails — not names or phone numbers.

🗺️

No GPS Access

We never request your device location. Location is typed text only. Even if compromised, nobody can pinpoint your physical position from our data.

🤖

AI Fraud Detection

Every post is reviewed by AI before going live. Fake, abusive, or suspicious posts are held for mod review — not published automatically.

🛡️

No Password Storage

We use OTP only. There is no password to steal. Even if our auth system were compromised, there are no password hashes to crack.

📂

Open Source Code

Every line of Karuna's code is public on GitHub. Anyone can audit it. Security through transparency, not obscurity.

The one rule for staying safe on Karuna
💡Never post your real name, phone number, or home address in a report. The platform protects your identity — but only if you do too. Write locations as landmarks, not GPS pins to your house.
